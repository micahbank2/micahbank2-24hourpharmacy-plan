Seasonal deadlines and content windows. Health/pharmacy traffic is highly seasonal. Missing a window costs real money.

January
  • Q4 estimated tax payment due Jan 15 Tax
  • ACA open enrollment ends Jan 15 - last push for insurance content Revenue
  • New Year's pharmacy hours article should already be live SEO
  • Post-holiday OTC clearance - Walgreens/CVS affiliate content, cold and flu aisle roundups Revenue
February-March
  • Flu season tail - pharmacy traffic still elevated SEO
  • Tax season - people searching for health deductions SEO
  • Write "Tax Deductions for Prescription Costs" article SEO
  • Valentines Day OTC/health gift guides (vitamins, blood pressure monitors, etc.) Revenue
April
  • Q1 estimated tax payment due Apr 15 Tax
  • Allergy season starts - pharmacy traffic uptick SEO
  • Publish allergy medication comparison content - OTC antihistamine affiliate articles (Claritin vs Zyrtec vs generic) Revenue
May-June
  • Q2 estimated tax payment due Jun 15 Tax
  • Summer travel pharmacy guide ("Finding a pharmacy on vacation") SEO
  • Review and update all city page pharmacy data (hours change seasonally) SEO
  • Sunscreen, bug spray, travel health kit guides - high summer OTC purchase intent Revenue
July-August
  • Back-to-school pharmacy needs (vaccinations, prescriptions) SEO
  • Publish supplement and vitamin buying guides before back-to-school season Revenue
  • Start writing ACA open enrollment guide NOW - needs to be indexed before October Revenue
  • Start writing Medicare Part D guide Revenue
  • Apply to MediaAlpha and EverQuote NOW so accounts are live before Oct spike Revenue
September
  • Q3 estimated tax payment due Sep 15 Tax
  • Publish all insurance/enrollment content - must be indexed before Oct Revenue
  • Medicare open enrollment starts Oct 15 - prep content NOW Revenue
  • Flu season starting - pharmacy traffic rising SEO
  • Publish flu shot guides, cold medicine comparisons - sets up affiliate revenue through winter Revenue
October-November (PEAK REVENUE)
  • ACA open enrollment begins Nov 1 - MediaAlpha/EverQuote insurance leads spike 10-20x. This is your highest revenue-per-visitor window of the year. Revenue
  • Medicare open enrollment: Oct 15 - Dec 7 Revenue
  • Flu season peak - highest pharmacy search volume of year SEO
  • Publish Thanksgiving pharmacy hours article (2 weeks before) SEO
  • Publish Christmas/New Year's pharmacy hours (by Dec 1) SEO
  • Holiday OTC gift guides (thermometers, blood pressure monitors, pill organizers) - Amazon Associates conversions spike Revenue
December
  • ACA enrollment continues through Jan 15 Revenue
  • Medicare enrollment ends Dec 7 Revenue
  • Holiday pharmacy hours - high traffic, high ad RPMs Revenue
  • Year-end: export metrics, review annual revenue, plan next year Tax
